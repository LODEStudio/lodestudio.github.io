LODE—Diversifying Wyoming’s Economy


Our Mission

At LODE, our mission is to develop a repeatable methodology for building successful innovative technology startups. Our studio brings ideas to life as standalone companies that we want to scale. Our culture comprises passion for people with enriching mentorship, while driving economic growth by creating meaningful jobs in Wyoming. We’re a Laramie-based startup revolutionizing business creation through the power of our startup studio business model.

We’re experienced entrepreneurs looking to build amazing startups with our product & engineering teams to bring our visions to life. We iterate quickly with a bias towards action, the ability to think in first principles, and the drive needed to excel in a startup environment.


“We’re creating new tech jobs in Wyoming. Building out the ecosystem, attracting follow-on capital, that’s what LODE is doing.”

— Rich Kopcho, Managing Director
